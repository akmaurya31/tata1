About Zudio Franchise

In Bangalore, the first Zudio location, approximately 8000 square feet, opened for business in September 2016. There are currently 352 outlets throughout India. Products from the Zudio franchise are available in the following categories: loungewear, footwear, kids’ clothing, men’s clothing, women’s clothing, and beauty items.

FOCO, short for Franchise Owned, Company Operated, is a particular kind of franchise agreement in which the franchisor keeps ownership of the business while letting a franchisee handle day-to-day operations. While the corporation supports and manages the franchisee’s business on their behalf, the franchisee invests it.
